About

I am an Associate Professor of Accounting at the Antai College of Economics and Management, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests span auditing, financial reporting, tax policy, and the real effects of accounting and legal institutions on firms and the broader economy.

I received my Ph.D. in Management (Accounting) from the National University of Singapore and hold undergraduate and graduate degrees in Accounting from Renmin University of China.
